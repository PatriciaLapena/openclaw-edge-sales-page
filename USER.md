## Profile

### Name / Brand:
Patricia Lapena — Founder of Reclaimed Vitality

### What I Do:
I help women 60+ reclaim their energy, health, and sense of purpose through simple mindset and nutrition shifts — and support them in rediscovering (or recreating) passion and meaningful income as they step into their next chapter.

### My Story:
My work comes from lived experience.

I spent years as a holistic nutrition practitioner helping others improve their health. But as I entered my 60s, I faced my own challenges — weight gain, high blood pressure, and a noticeable drop in energy.

Instead of accepting that as "just part of aging," I made intentional changes to how I was thinking, eating, and living. Over a short period of time, I lost 30 pounds, brought my blood pressure back to normal, and started to feel like myself again.

But what I didn't expect was the deeper shift — realizing that this stage of life isn't about slowing down, it's about redefining how we want to live.

Reclaimed Vitality was born from that transition — not just as a health-focused program, but as a space where women can rebuild their energy, reconnect with themselves, and create a next chapter that feels purposeful and alive.

### What I Offer:

- A 28-Day Reclaimed Vitality Reset (focused on mindset, nutrition, and daily habits)
- A supportive community for women navigating this stage of life
- A simple vitality assessment to help women understand where they are and what they need next
- Sister Wealth Creators — a second-tier experience within the community designed to help women explore or build aligned income streams, especially for those who want or need additional income in retirement

### Who I Serve:
Women 60+ who are navigating a new chapter — whether that's retirement, changes in health, or a shift in identity — and who feel like something is missing.

They are ready to feel better physically, think differently mentally, and explore what's still possible for them — including creating income, contribution, and connection in a way that fits this stage of life.

### Why Me:
I'm not teaching from theory — I've lived this transition myself.

My approach is grounded, realistic, and supportive. I focus on simple, sustainable shifts that create real change — not overwhelm.

What makes this different is that I don't stop at health.
I believe vitality also includes purpose, contribution, and the ability to create income if desired.

Reclaimed Vitality is not just a program — it's the beginning of a movement for women who are ready to redefine what aging looks and feels like, on their own terms.
