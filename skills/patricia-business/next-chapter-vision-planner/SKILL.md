# Next Chapter Vision Planner

Use this skill to turn the original dream-life exercise into a Patricia-branded experience that fits Reclaimed Vitality and Sister Wealth Creators.

## Core Positioning

Treat this as a **guided breakthrough exercise**, not a calculator.

The emotional sequence is:
1. help her imagine the life she wants now
2. reconnect her to desire, health, ease, beauty, and possibility
3. give that vision shape
4. optionally show what level of income would support it
5. invite her into the right next step

## Two Versions

### 1) Reclaimed Vitality Version
Use this version first for most prospects.

**Purpose**
Help women 60+ reconnect with what they want this next chapter to feel like.

**Tone**
- warm
- feminine
- calm
- hopeful
- elegant
- encouraging
- never aggressive

**Do not sound like:**
- hustle culture
- hard-closing sales
- macho performance coaching
- financial bro energy

**Best framing**
- next chapter
- renewed vitality
- freedom
- peace
- nourishment
- joy
- confidence
- purpose
- possibility

**Suggested opening**
"Let’s take a moment to imagine what you truly want this next chapter of life to look and feel like. Not what feels practical. Not what you’ve settled for. What do you actually want?"

**Suggested question flow**
Ask one at a time unless Patricia asks for a worksheet-style list.

1. What kind of home or environment would feel beautiful, peaceful, and supportive in this next chapter?
2. How do you want your body, energy, and health to feel day to day?
3. What kind of travel, experiences, or adventures do you want to enjoy?
4. What would nourishment look like for you — groceries, dining out, good food, ease in the kitchen?
5. How do you want your days to feel — spacious, social, creative, restful, purposeful?
6. What kind of support would make life easier or more enjoyable — housekeeping, wellness support, beauty, self-care, coaching, transportation?
7. What role do family, friendships, or giving play in the life you want?
8. What still wants to come through you in this season — contribution, legacy, creativity, business, leadership, or service?
9. Optional: what kind of monthly income would make this life feel fully supported?

**End state**
The user should feel inspired, seen, and ready for a next step.

**Soft CTA**
Use when appropriate:
"If this woke something up in you, Reclaimed Vitality is where we begin creating the energy, clarity, and support to help you step into it."

---

### 2) Sister Wealth Creators Version
Use this after the vision is clear or when the user explicitly wants the numbers.

**Purpose**
Translate a desired lifestyle into concrete monthly and daily income targets.

**Tone**
- grounded
- empowering
- practical
- still warm
- more specific and numbers-oriented

**Suggested question flow**
Collect exact monthly numbers for:
1. home
2. vehicle
3. travel
4. food and dining
5. wellness support
6. household support
7. family support
8. retirement and investments
9. extras
10. optional business-growth budget

If the user gives annual or per-trip numbers, convert them to monthly and tell them the converted amount.

### Calculations
- monthly total = sum of all categories
- yearly = monthly × 12
- weekly = monthly ÷ 4.33
- daily = monthly ÷ 30
- hourly = daily ÷ 8
- round to nearest cent

### Reveal format
Use plain bullets on chat platforms instead of markdown tables.

Example:
- Dream Home: $8,500.00
- Vehicle: $1,300.00
- Travel: $2,083.33
- Food & Dining: $3,000.00
- Wellness Support: $0.00
- Household Support: $0.00
- Family Support: $0.00
- Retirement & Investments: $2,000.00
- Other: $1,000.00

Then show:
- Monthly total
- Yearly total
- Weekly target
- Daily target
- Hourly target

**Bridge CTA**
"If you know you want more freedom, more income, and a real plan to create it, Sister Wealth Creators is where we help you turn that vision into strategy."

- renewal
- nourishment
- peace
- sisterhood
- hope
- calm strength

Prefer emotionally safe, elegant language over hype.

## Output Guidance

### When Patricia asks for a prospect-facing version
Default to the Reclaimed Vitality framing first.

### When Patricia asks for monetization or income-planning
Use the Sister Wealth Creators framing.

### When Patricia asks how to package it
Recommend these options in order:
1. lead magnet workbook
2. live Zoom or workshop exercise
3. nurture-email series follow-up
4. Sister Wealth Creators bridge worksheet

## Guardrails

- Do not give tax, legal, or investment advice.
- Do not make the exercise feel shaming or manipulative.
- Do not imply a woman is behind because of her age or income.
- Do not overemphasize hustle; emphasize possibility, clarity, and aligned action.
- Keep the conversation human, encouraging, and calm.
