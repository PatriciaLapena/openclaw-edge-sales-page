# Design Your Next Chapter - Follow-Up Email Sequence

## Purpose
Nurture new opt-ins after they join the **Design Your Next Chapter Leads** group.

Goal:
- build connection with Patricia
- encourage workbook completion
- deepen emotional resonance
- open the door to Reclaimed Vitality
- later segment for Sister Wealth Creators interest

---

## Email 1 - Welcome / Delivery
**Timing:** Immediately

**Goal:** Deliver the workbook and set tone

**Core message:**
- welcome them warmly
- affirm this season of life
- deliver workbook link
- encourage gentle reflection

CTA:
- Open the workbook

---

## Email 2 - You are allowed to want more
**Timing:** 1 day later

**Subject ideas:**
- You are allowed to want more
- This next chapter gets to be beautiful too
- A gentle reminder for you today

**Core message:**
Many women spend years putting themselves last.
This email reminds them that wanting peace, health, beauty, support, freedom, or a more meaningful life is not selfish.

CTA:
- Return to the workbook

---

## Email 3 - What do you really want now?
**Timing:** 3 days later

**Subject ideas:**
- What do you really want now?
- Not what’s practical — what do you want?
- A question worth answering honestly

**Core message:**
Invite them to revisit one or two workbook questions:
- home and environment
- health and energy
- purpose and possibility

CTA:
- Continue the workbook
- Reply and share what came up for them

---

## Email 4 - Sometimes clarity changes everything
**Timing:** 5 days later

**Subject ideas:**
- Sometimes clarity changes everything
- What became clear for you?
- One small truth can change a lot

**Core message:**
Highlight that awareness is powerful.
Some women realize they need:
- better health support
- more community
- more confidence
- more purpose
- more income freedom

CTA:
- Invite them to explore Reclaimed Vitality

---

## Email 5 - Your next chapter is not too late
**Timing:** 7 days later

**Subject ideas:**
- Your next chapter is not too late
- You are not behind
- It is not too late to feel alive again

**Core message:**
Close the sequence with a warm invitation into Reclaimed Vitality.
For those who resonated with the money side, note that future guidance around aligned income and possibility is also coming.

CTA:
- Join Reclaimed Vitality
- Or reply if they want support choosing their next step
