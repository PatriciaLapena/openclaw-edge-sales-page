# Next Chapter Vision Packaging

## Purpose
Turn the original Dream Life concept into a prospect-friendly journey for Patricia Lapena's businesses:
- **Reclaimed Vitality** = emotional lifestyle vision + renewed possibility
- **Sister Wealth Creators** = income planning + business-building bridge

This should be positioned as a guided experience, not just a calculator.

---

## Core Strategy

### Phase 1: Reclaimed Vitality Front-End
Use a warm, reflective version to help women 60+ imagine the life they want in their next chapter.

**Primary promise:**
Get clear on what you want this season of life to feel like — in your home, health, energy, freedom, and purpose.

**Suggested names:**
- Your Next Chapter Vision
- Design Your Next Chapter
- The Reclaimed Vitality Life Vision Exercise
- What Would Your Next Chapter Look Like?

**Best use cases:**
- lead magnet PDF
- workshop exercise
- email nurture entry point
- community challenge prompt

**Primary CTA:**
If this vision woke something up in you, Reclaimed Vitality is where we begin creating the energy, clarity, and support to help you step into it.

---

### Phase 2: Sister Wealth Creators Back-End
Once a woman has named the life she wants, introduce the practical money conversation.

**Primary promise:**
Translate your dream lifestyle into a clear monthly income target, then identify what kind of offer, business, or income stream could support it.

**Suggested names:**
- The Next Chapter Income Planner
- What Would It Take to Fund the Life You Want?
- Sister Wealth Creators Vision-to-Income Planner
- The Dream Life Income Calculator

**Best use cases:**
- paid workshop
- Sister Wealth Creators onboarding tool
- application exercise
- bridge offer after Reclaimed Vitality

**Primary CTA:**
If you know you want more freedom, more income, and a real plan to create it, Sister Wealth Creators is where we help you build that next chapter.

---

## Funnel Flow

1. **Attract** with a vision-based message
   - "What do you want this next chapter of life to actually look like?"
2. **Guide** them through the emotional vision first
   - home, freedom, energy, travel, nourishment, joy, contribution
3. **Reveal** that their desired life has a shape and a cost
4. **Invite** them into the right next step:
   - Reclaimed Vitality if they need health, clarity, support, and momentum
   - Sister Wealth Creators if they want income creation and business building

---

## Reclaimed Vitality Version Structure

### Tone
- warm
- hopeful
- feminine
- supportive
- elegant
- calm
- possibility-driven

### What to avoid
- hustle language
- hard sales energy
- aggressive coaching
- "go grind" type framing

### Suggested question categories
1. Your ideal home and environment
2. How you want your body, energy, and health to feel
3. Travel and experiences you want to enjoy
4. Food, nourishment, and daily lifestyle
5. Time freedom and how you want your days to look
6. Self-care, beauty, and support
7. Family, giving, and relationships
8. Purpose, contribution, and what still wants to come through you
9. Optional: what kind of income would support this life?

### End result
A woman finishes feeling:
- seen
- inspired
- reconnected to desire
- open to change
- curious about the next step

---

## Sister Wealth Creators Version Structure

### Tone
- grounded
- aspirational
- empowering
- practical
- clear

### Suggested question categories
1. Dream home monthly cost
2. Vehicle monthly cost
3. Travel budget
4. Food and dining
5. Wellness support (trainer, massage, supplements, etc.)
6. Household support (cleaner, chef, help)
7. Family support or legacy giving
8. Retirement and investments
9. Lifestyle extras
10. Business growth budget (optional)

### End result
A woman finishes with:
- monthly target
- yearly target
- weekly target
- daily target
- a clearer reason to create income
- a natural entry point into Sister Wealth Creators

**Design Your Next Chapter**

### Subtitle options
- A gentle guided exercise for women 60+ who are ready to reclaim vitality, clarity, and possibility
- Get clear on the life you want now — and what it may take to create it
