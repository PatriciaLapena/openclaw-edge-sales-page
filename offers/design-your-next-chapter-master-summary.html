Design Your Next Chapter

Master Project Summary for Reclaimed Vitality

What It Is

A lead magnet funnel for women 60+ that helps them reflect on the life they want in their next chapter, think about home, health, energy, freedom, purpose, and support, estimate what that lifestyle may take financially, and then be gently led toward Reclaimed Vitality.

The core idea: this is not just a worksheet. It is a guided journey through emotional reflection, clarity around desire, gentle number awareness, and a next-step invitation.

What Was Built

  • Lead capture page — collects first name and email before access
  • Thank-you page — welcomes the lead and guides them to the workbook
  • Gated workbook page — online workbook with visible questions and answer boxes
  • Built-in calculator — automatically totals monthly, yearly, weekly, daily, and hourly numbers
  • PDF backup version — downloadable workbook for saving or printing
  • MailerLite lead group — Design Your Next Chapter Leads
  • Welcome email — warm first email delivering the workbook
  • Full 5-email nurture sequence — gently leads toward Reclaimed Vitality
  • MailerLite-ready email version — cleaner formatting for automation
  • Reclaimed Vitality destination page copy — future conversion page copy
  • Setup documents — link structure, MailerLite setup instructions, and implementation checklist
