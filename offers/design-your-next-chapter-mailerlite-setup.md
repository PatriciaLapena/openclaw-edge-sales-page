## Best Practice Notes

### 1. Keep the workbook link in every early email
This increases completion and helps leads re-enter the funnel if they leave.

### 2. Do not over-sell too early
This audience responds better to warmth, trust, reflection, and gentle momentum.

### 3. Let Reclaimed Vitality be the first main offer
The workbook opens the emotional door. Reclaimed Vitality is the first natural invitation.

### 4. Use Sister Wealth Creators later
Only after the relationship is built or when income/freedom language clearly resonates.
