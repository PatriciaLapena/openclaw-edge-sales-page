## What Reclaimed Vitality Is
Reclaimed Vitality is a supportive space for women 60+ who are ready to:
- improve their energy and overall well-being
- reconnect with mindset, nourishment, and daily habits that truly support them
- feel more confident in their body and in this season of life
- rediscover purpose, possibility, and what still feels meaningful
- be part of a warm community of women walking a similar path

This is not about perfection.
It is not about harsh rules.
And it is not about pretending aging means your best years are behind you.

It is about simple shifts, real support, and a more honest, alive, nourishing way of living.

---

## Who It Is For
Reclaimed Vitality is for women 60+ who:
- know something in life needs to shift
- are tired of feeling flat, heavy, disconnected, or uncertain
- want better energy, health, and mindset without overwhelm
- are craving community, encouragement, and a fresh sense of possibility
- feel that this season of life could hold more than they have been allowing themselves to imagine
