## Core Brand Essence

Reclaimed Vitality should feel like:
- renewal
- nourishment
- peace
- sisterhood
- hope
- healing
- beauty
- calm strength
- next chapter energy
- “it’s not too late”
- “this is your time”

## Emotional Tone

Use a tone that feels:
- warm
- feminine
- elegant
- encouraging
- hopeful
- supportive
- calm
- uplifting
- human
- relational

Avoid tone that feels:
- corporate
- clinical
- cold
- harsh
- flashy
- too young
- too trendy
- overdesigned
- aggressive/salesy

## Visual Direction

### Strong themes
- women 60+ thriving
- friendship and community
- renewal and blooming
- peace and grounding
- nourishment and real food
- heart-centered vitality
- morning light / sunrise / fresh start
- gentle luxury / soft elegance

### Best design feel
- clean and spacious
- soft feminine styling
- polished but human
- elegant without fussiness
- vibrant in a gentle way
- emotionally safe and welcoming

### Good graphic motifs
- subtle hearts
- soft florals
- leaves / natural curves
- sunrise light
- circular / flowing shapes
- gentle vitality motifs

Do **not** overuse medical or clinical imagery.
Heartbeat/life-force imagery can inspire supporting motifs, but should not make the brand feel like a medical practice.

## Color Direction

Preferred palette direction:
- blush pink
- mauve / dusty rose
- soft lavender
- warm cream
- soft black / charcoal
- warm neutral tones
- sunrise gold accents
- fresh natural food colors used sparingly

Overall palette should feel soft, elevated, and warm rather than loud.

## Logo Direction

Current best-fit logo direction:
- elegant wordmark
- subtle feminine accent or symbol
- possibly a soft leaf / flourish / heart-inspired detail
- mature and readable
- beautiful, calm, trustworthy

Avoid:
- cluttered symbols
- overly literal medical icons
- trendy startup-style marks
- anything too busy or cartoonish

## Document/PDF Styling Guidance

For Patricia-facing PDFs and branded documents:
- always prefer PDF over Markdown as final deliverable
- use generous white space
- use soft accent colors
- pair elegant script accents with readable body fonts
- keep layouts clean and uncluttered
- use uplifting headings
- include real-woman / wellness / nourishment visuals when appropriate
- make materials feel encouraging and easy to open/use on phone

## Working Summary

Reclaimed Vitality is a brand for women 60+ centered on:
- reclaiming energy
- reclaiming health
- reclaiming peace
- reclaiming purpose
- stepping into a beautiful next chapter

It is not just a health brand. It is a blend of wellness, renewal, sisterhood, nourishment, hope, and meaningful new beginnings.
